关于我~About Me

A small introduction about my self

张少魁 Shao-Kui Zhang 장소규

shaokui@tsinghua.edu.cn

Shao-Kui is currently a Shuimu Scholar postdoc researcher in Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, Department of CS & Tech, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of A/Prof Song-Hai Zhang. Shao-Kui received his Doctor degree in Department of CS & Tech, Tsinghua University, China in 2023. Shao-Kui received his Bachelor degree in Software College, Northeastern University, China in 2018.

His current research interest includes Computer Graphics, 3D Scene Layout, Synthesis and Interaction.

Shao-Kui has served as a teaching assistant of course Data Visualization each spring semester, mainly for tutoring coding for Data Visualization. The related code received over 400 stars in Github. The related tutorial received over 121K views in sum.

He is also a core developer of an open-source 3D scene platform founded by the National Key Technology R&D Program.
